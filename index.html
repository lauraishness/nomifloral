What I Do

Nomi Floral provides floral design for events and individuals in Northern Colorado. These designs draw from the inherent natural, wild, and romantic qualities of flowers and plants. As such, I follow less traditional models for structured floral design, and instead participate with the abundance of nature, co-creating beauty from the chaotic joy of nature's regenerative impulses.

Her Story

My grandmother, Norma "Nomi" inspires my work. A southern rose who grew up in the oil fields of eastern Texas and western Louisiana, Nomi appreciated beauty in the midst of life's hardness. My childhood adventures in her expansive back-yard gardens and rosebeds rooted in me a deep love for flowers and floral design. While Nomi passed away peacefully after a long, full life at 93, her legacy lives on in our love and in this work.
