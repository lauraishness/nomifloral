What I Do

Nomi Floral provides floral design for events and individuals in Northern Colorado. These designs draw from the inherent natural, wild, and romantic qualities of flowers and plants. As such, I follow less traditional models for structured floral design, and instead participate with the abundance of nature, co-creating beauty from the chaotic joy of nature's regenerative impulses.
